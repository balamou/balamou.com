The Idea

An iOS game that gathered over 3000 users in a span of a month

Tools

Objective-C
Adobe Photoshop

After publishing my first iOS app to the app store (Carbon12), my friend Daesol Jung reached to me with a new game idea. In the era of games like flappy bird and 2048 we had no shortage of our own. Daesol came with an idea of a game where you connect numbers that all add up and show in the last circle. I designed the initial prototype in photoshop and developed the rest in Objective-C. After a few iterations the final game looked like this:


The Outcome

  • After we published the game in May 2014 the app got around a thousand users in its first month
  • The second month the user growth reached its peak at 3000 total users
  • The game is not currently on the app store since, it was first designed for iPhone 4 (which had no constraint system yet)
